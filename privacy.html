Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

Introduction

At YumWhirl, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal identification information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you register on our site, subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a form, or interact with our services. This information is used to personalize your experience and provide the services you request. We only collect this information when voluntarily submitted by you.

2. Non-Personal Identification Information

We automatically collect non-personal information about your device and browsing actions when you interact with our website. This may include browser type, operating system, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data. This information helps us improve our website and understand user behavior without identifying individual users.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze trends, administer the website, track movements around the site, and gather demographic information. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our services.

4. Payment Information

When you make purchases through our site, we collect payment information such as credit card numbers, expiration dates, and security codes. This sensitive information is encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers after transaction completion.

5. User-Generated Content

If you submit recipes, comments, reviews, or other content to our site, we collect and store this information. This content may be displayed publicly along with your username or other identifying information you choose to provide. We reserve the right to moderate and remove inappropriate content.

6. Social Media Integration

When you interact with our content through social media platforms or use social media features on our site, we may receive information from those platforms including your profile information and any other information you permit the social network to share with third parties.

7. Third-Party Analytics

We use third-party analytics services like Google Analytics to help analyze how users use our site. These services use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about website use which is used to compile statistical reports on website activity and improve our services.

8. Location Data

With your permission, we may collect and process information about your actual location when you use our mobile applications or access our website from mobile devices. We use various technologies to determine location, including IP address, GPS, and other sensors.

How We Use Your Information

9. To Provide and Improve Services

We use collected information to operate, maintain, and improve our website and services. This includes personalizing your experience, developing new features, and ensuring content is presented in the most effective manner for you and your device. Your data helps us understand user preferences and optimize our offerings.

10. Communication Purposes

We may use your email address to send you information and updates pertaining to your account, respond to inquiries, and provide customer service. With your consent, we may also send newsletters, marketing materials, and other information that may be of interest to you.

11. Transaction Processing

Your information is used to process transactions, send order confirmations, and provide customer support related to purchases. This includes verifying your identity, processing payments, and delivering purchased goods or services. We may also use this information to prevent fraudulent transactions.

12. Research and Analytics

We analyze user behavior and preferences to understand how our services are used, improve content and functionality, and develop new products and services. This analysis helps us identify trends, measure effectiveness, and make data-driven decisions to enhance user experience.

13. Legal Compliance and Protection

We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests. This includes protecting our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

14. Marketing and Advertising

With your consent, we may use your information to provide targeted advertisements, promotions, and special offers that may be of interest to you. This includes displaying ads on our site and through third-party platforms based on your browsing behavior and preferences.

15. Account Management

We use your information to administer your account, verify your identity, provide technical support, and notify you about changes to our services. This ensures the security of your account and helps us provide efficient customer service when needed.

16. Community Features

For interactive features like comments, forums, or recipe sharing, we use your information to facilitate participation, display your contributions (with attribution you choose), and maintain community guidelines. This fosters engagement while protecting all users' experience.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

17. Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors, or agents who perform services for us or on our behalf. These include payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, customer service, and marketing assistance. They only receive necessary information to perform their functions.

18. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or other sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your information.

19. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas). This includes responding to legal process, protecting our rights, investigating fraud, or ensuring the safety of our users and the public.

20. Affiliates

We may share your information with our affiliates, in which case we will require those affiliates to honor this Privacy Policy. Affiliates include our parent company and any subsidiaries, joint venture partners, or other companies that we control or that are under common control with us.

21. Business Partners

With your consent, we may share your information with business partners to offer you certain products, services, or promotions. These partners are prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than those we specify and must provide the same level of protection as outlined in this policy.

22. Aggregate or De-identified Data

We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you for research, marketing, analytics, or other business purposes. This data helps industry partners understand trends without compromising individual privacy.

23. Publicly Shared Information

Any information you choose to share in public areas of the website (comments, forums, recipe submissions) becomes publicly available and may be read, collected, and used by others. We cannot control how others may use this information once it's made public.

24. With Your Consent

We may disclose your personal information for any other purpose with your explicit consent. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us, though this will not affect any processing that has already taken place.

Data Security

25. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of your personal information. These include encryption, access controls, secure servers, firewalls, and regular security audits. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

26. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and its purpose, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

27. Password Protection

Where we have given you (or you have chosen) a password for access to certain parts of our website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. We advise against sharing passwords and recommend using strong, unique passwords for your accounts.

28. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that compromises your personal information, we will notify you and applicable regulators when we are legally required to do so. Our notification will include information about what was compromised and steps you can take to protect yourself.

29. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any website you visit via links from our site.

Your Rights and Choices

30. Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to request correction of any inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information. You can review and update certain account information by logging into your account settings.

31. Data Portability

Where applicable, you may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. You may also request that we transmit this data directly to another organization where technically feasible.

32. Deletion Rights

You may request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances, such as when the data is no longer necessary for the purposes collected or if you withdraw consent. Note that we may retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

33. Opt-Out Options

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications or adjusting your account settings. Even if you opt out, we may still send you transactional or relationship messages.

34. Cookie Controls

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually modify your browser settings to decline or delete cookies, but this may affect certain features of our website. We provide cookie preference tools to manage your choices.

35. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers. However, you can control tracking through our cookie settings and opt-out of certain third-party tracking through their respective privacy policies.

36. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians may contact us regarding any concerns.

37. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the CCPA, including rights to know about personal information collected, disclosed, or sold, and the right to request deletion of personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA.

38. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing. You may also withdraw consent or lodge complaints with a supervisory authority.

Policy Updates

39. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on our site with a new "Last Updated" date. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

40. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@yumwhirl.com. You may also write to us at the physical address provided in our footer. We will respond to all legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe.
