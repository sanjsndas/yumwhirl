Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own recipe to YumWhirl?

We love receiving recipe submissions from our community! You can submit your recipes through our online submission form on the "Contribute" page. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and may contact you for additional details. Please ensure your recipe is original and includes all necessary ingredients, measurements, and step-by-step instructions.

What's the best way to contact customer support?

For fastest service, please use our contact form above or email help@yumwhirl.com. Include as many details as possible about your issue. Our average response time is 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call our support line at +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours.

Do you offer nutrition information for all recipes?

We provide nutritional estimates for most recipes on our platform. These calculations are based on standard ingredients and serving sizes. However, actual nutritional content may vary based on specific brands, preparation methods, and portion sizes. For precise dietary needs, we recommend consulting a nutritionist.

Can I use YumWhirl recipes for my food blog or business?

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we encourage sharing with proper attribution. For personal blogs, you may adapt our recipes with credit and a link back to the original. For commercial use (including restaurants, meal kits, or packaged foods), please contact our partnerships team at biz@yumwhirl.com for licensing information.

How do I report an error in a recipe?

We appreciate help catching any errors in our recipes. Please email corrections@yumwhirl.com with the recipe title, specific error details, and (if possible) the correct information. Our culinary team will review and update the recipe as needed. You can also use the "Report Error" button on each recipe page.

Are all YumWhirl recipes gluten-free/dairy-free/vegan?

We offer a variety of recipes to accommodate different dietary needs, but not all recipes are allergen-free. Each recipe clearly labels dietary attributes (gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, etc.) and often includes substitution suggestions. Use our advanced filters to find recipes matching your dietary requirements.

How often do you add new recipes to the site?

Our culinary team publishes new recipes weekly, with seasonal collections added throughout the year. Subscribers to our newsletter receive first access to new recipes. We typically add 10-15 new recipes each week across various categories including quick meals, meal prep, and special diets.

Do you offer cooking classes or in-person events?

Yes! We host virtual cooking classes monthly and occasional in-person events in major cities. Check our "Events" page for upcoming opportunities. We also partner with culinary schools and chefs for special workshops. Corporate team-building cooking events can be arranged by contacting events@yumwhirl.com.

Can I request a specific type of recipe?

Absolutely! We welcome recipe requests from our community. Use our "Recipe Request" form or email requests@yumwhirl.com with details about what you're looking for (ingredients, dietary needs, occasion, etc.). While we can't fulfill every request, popular suggestions often inspire future recipe development.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your account settings. Navigate to "Billing" and follow the prompts to cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. For assistance, contact billing@yumwhirl.com. We'd appreciate hearing any feedback about why you're canceling.

What's your policy on sponsored content?

We occasionally partner with brands we trust to create sponsored recipes or content. All sponsored content is clearly labeled as such. We maintain full editorial control over recipe development and only promote products we genuinely recommend. For partnership inquiries, please contact partnerships@yumwhirl.com.

How accurate are the preparation time estimates?

Our time estimates are based on testing by multiple home cooks of varying skill levels. Times include active preparation but assume basic kitchen equipment and familiarity with techniques. Your experience may vary based on skill, kitchen setup, and multitasking. We welcome feedback on time estimates.

Do you have an affiliate program?

Yes, we have an affiliate program for qualified food bloggers, influencers, and content creators. Participants earn commission on qualifying sales generated through their unique links. Applications are reviewed based on content quality and audience alignment. Apply through our "Affiliates" page or email affiliates@yumwhirl.com.

Why don't you list calorie counts for all recipes?

While we provide nutritional information for most recipes, we intentionally don't emphasize calorie counts as we promote a balanced, non-diet approach to eating. Our nutrition data focuses on protein, fiber, and key nutrients. For specific calorie needs, we recommend consulting the detailed nutrition panel available on each recipe.

How can I advertise on YumWhirl?

We offer several advertising opportunities for food-related brands. Our media kit with rates and audience demographics is available upon request. For display ads, sponsored content, or newsletter placements, please contact sales@yumwhirl.com. We carefully vet all advertisers to ensure alignment with our values.

Are your recipes tested before publishing?

Every YumWhirl recipe undergoes rigorous testing by our culinary team. Recipes are tested multiple times in home kitchens (not test kitchens) to ensure they work for real cooks. We evaluate for clarity, accuracy, and reliability. Our testers include cooks of varying skill levels to catch potential issues.

Can I republish YumWhirl recipes in my cookbook?

Republishing our recipes requires written permission. For personal projects (like family cookbooks), we typically grant permission with proper attribution. For commercial publications, licensing fees apply. Contact copyright@yumwhirl.com with details about your project, including which recipes you'd like to use and your planned distribution.

Do you offer services for meal planning?

Our premium members have access to customizable meal plans with shopping lists. For personalized meal planning services (including specialized diets), we recommend our certified nutrition partners. Email mealplans@yumwhirl.com for recommendations based on your specific needs and budget.

How do you handle food safety concerns?

Food safety is paramount in all our recipes. We follow USDA guidelines for safe cooking temperatures and handling. Recipes include necessary safety notes for raw ingredients. For specific concerns (allergies, immunocompromised individuals), we recommend consulting the recipe notes or contacting safety@yumwhirl.com for clarification.

Can I interview someone from YumWhirl for my publication?

We're happy to participate in media interviews with advance notice. Please send your request to press@yumwhirl.com including your publication, topic, deadline, and preferred interview format. Our PR team will connect you with the appropriate spokesperson. For urgent requests, please call +1 (555) 987-6543.
